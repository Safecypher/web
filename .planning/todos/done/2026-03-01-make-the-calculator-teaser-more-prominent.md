## Problem

The homepage calculator teaser widget (DemoFormSection) exists and funnels visitors to the portal calculator, but it may not be visually prominent enough to drive conversions. Visitors may miss or overlook the CTA before scrolling past it.

## Solution

Review placement, size, and visual weight of the teaser section on the homepage. Consider: moving it higher on the page, increasing contrast, adding a stronger value proposition headline, or making the input + CTA more visually distinct. Could also A/B test prominence changes via PostHog feature flags.
